Natural vs Unnatural Stress:

Your body’s stress response, including the ‘fight-or-flight’ response, was designed for survival out in the wilderness. In modern society, though, that mechanism is needed less and less. However, modern society also introduces new ‘unnatural’ stressors. Importantly, they are present all throughout high school and college. The issue is that while natural stressors are resolved quickly, such as by running away from a predator, the stress of an upcoming essay or afterschool job can last weeks or more.

A constant state of stress can disrupt your brain’s ability to handle stress and over time, other parts of your brain’s functioning get disrupted too. Eventually, chronic stress will lead to your brain structure literally morphing into a state aligned with depression. The altered brain loses the ability to handle stress more and more, and symptoms of ‘burnout’ emerge.
