Small Steps

I know. It’s not fair. It sucks and if you are suffering from burnout it may be hard to ‘take action.’ However, no matter the situation, anyone can start with this one small step: acknowledge what you just learned. ‘Stress causes depression.’ In the end, all of the research boils down to this one statement.

True, sometimes stress is necessary and unavoidable when trying to be successful; it may not be feasible to snap and make that afterschool sports less demanding. However, maybe you can find a less-apparent source of stress that has been bothering you lately. That one school assignment that you have been procrastinating? Just try working on it for at least a little bit. Is it hard to mentally unwind after coming home at the end of the day? Try to find a healthy habit or routine to do at the end of each day which helps you deal with the stress endured from the day.

There is no magical solution, but at least you are aware of how harmful stress truly is, and know how to find ways to manage it in your own situation.
